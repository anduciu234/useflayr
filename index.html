What Flayr does

Flayr creates and manages advertising creative for your brand.

Flayr is an AI advertising platform for ecommerce, app, and SaaS brands. It studies a brand's products, audience, positioning, and competitor advertising to create ready-to-publish video and image ads. Users can edit those creatives, organize them in an ad library, and launch or monitor campaigns across supported advertising platforms.

Understand your brand Analyze your website, products, audience, tone, and competitive market.
Create ad creatives Generate, edit, and organize product photos, UGC videos, and custom ad formats.
Launch and improve Send approved creatives to supported ad platforms and monitor campaign performance.
Google Sign-In is used only to let users securely create and access their Flayr account. Flayr does not access Google Ads, Gmail, Drive, or other Google services through Sign-In.
